ABOUT THIS EVENT Augusta carols brings to you the greatest opportunity to explore your singing talent. Solo singing by All.

Singing Contest Rules

  • Every Participants will be judged individually
  • Songs should be in English or தமிழ் or മലയാളം
  • Music Instruments can be used (optional)

  • Last date of submission is 30th Nov,2021
  • Donot upload your song videos to any public platforms like Youtube/facebook etc before 25th Dec 2021.

  • Judges decisions are final
